📰 Community Bulletin

Summer Events Season

The city council is proud to announce a packed summer calendar for 2026. From live music at Downtown Hall to outdoor yoga in the park, there is something for every resident. Early registration is strongly encouraged as seats fill quickly.

New Venue: The Community Hub

Our newest venue, The Community Hub, opens in July in the Suburb district. It will host workshops, cooking masterclasses, and family afternoons all summer. Parking is available on site and public transport links are excellent.

Volunteering Opportunities

We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help run events. Whether you assist at the door, coordinate activities, or support accessibility needs, your contribution makes a real difference. Sign up via the registration form above.

Accessibility Updates

All 2026 events have been reviewed to ensure full accessibility. Designated parking, ramp access, and hearing loop systems are available at all major venues. Contact the council for specific requirements ahead of your event.
